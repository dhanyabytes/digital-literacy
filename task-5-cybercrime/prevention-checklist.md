Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

✅ General Safety Tips

✔️ Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts and enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
✔️ Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone — even if they claim to be from a bank or official authority  
✔️ Always verify links before clicking; avoid suspicious emails, messages, or unknown attachments  
✔️ Keep your phone, apps, and antivirus software updated to protect against malware  
✔️ Use secure Wi-Fi networks; avoid accessing sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi  
✔️ Review privacy settings on social media and limit what you share publicly  
✔️ Log out from devices after use, especially on shared or public systems  
✔️ Be cautious of job scams, fake internships, and offers that seem “too good to be true”

💰 Financial / UPI Safety Tips

✔️ Never approve unknown payment requests on UPI apps (like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)  
✔️ Remember: Receiving money does NOT require entering a PIN — only sending money does  
✔️ Double-check the recipient’s UPI ID or QR code before making any payment  
✔️ Do not scan random QR codes shared via WhatsApp or social media

🚨 Report Cybercrime

 Report online fraud at: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
 Helpline Number: 1930 (India’s Cyber Fraud Helpline)

Tip:

Stay alert, stay informed — most cybercrimes succeed due to small mistakes.
